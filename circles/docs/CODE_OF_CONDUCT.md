# Kamooni Code of Conduct

Kamooni is a pilot community platform. Verification is based on a simple trust agreement about how people use the space.

## Core expectations

- Be truthful. Represent yourself honestly and do not knowingly share false or misleading information.
- Be respectful. Treat other people with dignity and avoid harassment, hateful behavior, intimidation, or abuse.
- Be constructive. Participate in good faith and aim to make conversations and collaboration useful.
- Respect privacy. Do not share private or sensitive information about others without their consent.
- No spam or manipulation. Do not use Kamooni for scams, spam, coordinated deception, exploitation, or other manipulative behavior.
- Follow circle-specific rules where they apply, as long as they do not conflict with platform standards.

## Enforcement

Kamooni admins may review, limit, or restrict accounts that break this agreement or make the platform less safe, less trustworthy, or less useful for others.
